# Demo Video Script

Max length: 60 seconds

Goal: show the product experience and customer-to-merchant flow. Keep the recording focused on the app, not the code.

## Shot Plan

### 0-5s: Open On Map

Show the app on the `Map` screen with the larger Spark logo, profile menu, Google Maps, current location readout, and Spark floating agent.

Voiceover:

"This is Spark City Wallet, a Living Wallet that generates local offers from the user's real-time context instead of showing a static coupon list."

### 5-15s: Show Live Context

Tap `Simulate` if needed and show the context card: location, time, visible reasons, source evidence, and Spark status.

Voiceover:

"Spark reads the local moment: where the user is, time, weather, nearby places, opening status, local events, and demand signals."

### 15-25s: Generate Or Open Offer

Use `Ask Spark` with a simple prompt like "I want a coffee, find me deals", then open the generated offer.

Voiceover:

"The private intent is inferred locally, then Spark asks the agent for public deal intelligence and creates a specific time-limited offer."

### 25-35s: Accept And QR

Tap accept and show the QR/token redemption screen.

Voiceover:

"Accepting the offer issues a one-time checkout token. The redemption flow validates the QR proof and updates the wallet ledger."

### 35-45s: Graph Preview And Advanced Graph

Return to `Map`, show the mini graph preview, then tap `Open advanced graph` and briefly show the clustered canvas.

Voiceover:

"The wallet also builds a private local graph of routines, places, preferences, prompts, and offer outcomes. Users can inspect it and control it."

### 45-55s: Business Mode

Open the profile menu, switch to business mode, and show the merchant dashboard with Caffè Nero, radius map, stats, events, and Spark recommendation button.

Voiceover:

"On the merchant side, businesses set goals and guardrails, refresh local event intelligence, and use Spark recommendations to tune campaigns."

### 55-60s: Close

Show the active generated campaign or performance metrics.

Voiceover:

"Spark closes the loop: context detection, AI-generated offer, QR checkout, wallet cashback, and aggregate merchant analytics."

## Recording Notes

- Keep taps slow and deliberate.
- Do not spend more than two seconds on any one static card.
- Use the customer account first, then switch to business mode near the end.
- If an offer is already generated, use it rather than waiting for a new one.
- Make sure the video ends before 60 seconds.
